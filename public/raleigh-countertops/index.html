AG Stones Raleigh, NC

The impressive 40,000+ square foot showroom and slab gallery of AG Stones, Raleigh is situated at 1251 Burma Dr in Apex City. Our cutting-edge facility houses meticulously curated hard surface galleries, showcasing an extensive collection of stone slabs meticulously hand-selected worldwide. We welcome the public from Monday to Saturday for selection purposes.

Our diverse inventory caters to every taste and budget. From exotic stone to engineered materials, we offer the finest quality granite, marble, onyx, quartzite, quartz, porcelain, semi-precious stones, and more. Explore our extensive collection online or visit our showroom today. Our knowledgeable and experienced team of stone professionals is ready to assist you with all your project needs and inquiries.
